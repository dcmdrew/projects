How rebirth works

  • You earn rebirth points (RP) from lifetime coins each run.
  • Rebirthing resets run progress (coins and upgrades) but keeps RP.
  • Each RP gives +10% production permanently for future runs.
  • Requirement scales after each rebirth for long-term progression.
